Our Story

Nestled in the mountains near Mutare, Fountain Creek is a family-owned coffee farm offering exceptional coffee, breathtaking views, and unforgettable experiences. Discover the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure in Zimbabwe's beautiful Eastern Highlands.
